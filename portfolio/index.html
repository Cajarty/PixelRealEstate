Evolving Digital Canvas

PixelProperty is an evolving public art canvas where owners and users join together to play, design and trade.

WHAT IS PIXEL PROPERTY?

PixelProperty will be the world's first physical art canvas built using blockchain technology. The digital canvas is fully community-driven, powered by people coming together to play, create and share. Artists and advertisers alike are rewarded in our cryptocurrency, PixelPropertyToken, for contributing to the canvas’ organic growth. The canvas itself is built up of 10,000 regions, represented by earnable cryptocollectable tokens known as Properties.

    • Additional updates to the website to create a moderator panel.
