Evolving Digital Canvas

PixelProperty is an evolving public art canvas where owners and users work together to play, design and trade.

WHAT IS PIXEL PROPERTY?

PixelProperty will be the world's first physical art canvas built using blockchain technology. PixelProperty is composed of customizable Digital Properties that allow users and owners to share, advertise, and earn.

PixelPropertyToken (PPT) is awarded for your contributions to the canvas, a cryptocurrency used to power the Digital Property ecosystem. You are in control of your Properties and Tokens—trade and track ownership through the power of blockchain technology.

FEATURES

DRAW • UPLOAD • SHARE

Draw your pictures or upload images and share them with the world! All visual expressions are always welcome, from an individual's digital art to an organization's visual display.

CONNECT • NETWORK • ADVERTISE

Your digital properties will advertise your personal message and website. As an owner, you have some extra control over the property, and are rewarded for sharing it with the public.

BUY • TRADE • SELL

You can buy properties in either Ethereum or PixelPropertyToken. Properties can be traded with or sold to other users directly using PPT.

PLAY & REWARDS

The digital canvas is similar to a visual arcade, where it can cost PPT to play, and you may receive some tokens back for your consistent efforts in advancing the display.

GETTING STARTED

WHAT DO I NEED TO USE PIXELPROPERTY?

  • A computer runnning Brave, FireFox, or Google Chrome
  • MetaMask browser extension installed
  • Ether (ETH)

INSTALLING METAMASK

PixelProperty uses MetaMask to communicate with the digital real estate market, which is run on a Smart Contract through Ethereum. MetaMask is a digital wallet for Ethereum, meaning your funds are stored securely in this wallet, outside of PixelProperty.io. MetaMask is a wallet, protect it! Your MetaMask passwords or seed words must be kept secret, but don’t lose them.

We suggest you print off a paper copy of your password and seed words, and store them somewhere for safe keeping. These cannot be recovered if you lose them.

Install MetaMask

HOW DO I GET ETHER (ETH)?

For U.S. citizens, ETH can be bought within MetaMask itself.

For non U.S. citizens, you will have to buy it from an exchange. Exchanges such as Coinbase, Quadriga, and Kraken.

Note: PixelProperty is not affiliated with any of the listed exchanges.

HOW DO I SEND ETH TO METAMASK?

Once you have acquired ETH on the exchange of your choice, you must send it to your MetaMask ETH address.

You can find your MetaMask ETH address in the MetaMask extension by clicking on the “...” menu button, then selecting “Copy To Clipboard”. Provide this copied address where requested to receive funds from a market or other address.

TECHNICAL DETAILS

  1. PixelProperty is an Ethereum Smart Contract powered by the Ethereum network
  2. PixelProperty follows a sustainable income model rather than an ICO

PROPERTY DETAILS

  1. Each Digital Property is a unique, non-fungible token supported by Ethereum
  2. Users can set the display image, hover texts, and hyperlink of properties
  3. Properties can be traded between users at any price, where a 2% fee will be charged to sustain monthly costs
  4. Owners of Properties may advertise or earn PPT by allowing the public to use their Property
  5. There are 10,000 unique Properties within the PixelProperty market. No new Properties can ever be created or destroyed
  6. Each Property will be available for purchase at launch in either PPT or ETH. These Properties are known as System Properties
  7. System-Properties can be purchased on a sliding scale between ETH and PPT, increasing independently after each sale
  8. The System-Property price is the going rate for all System-Properties. Buying one System-Property will increase the price for all other System-Properties
  9. System-Properties are converted into User-Properties once purchased
  10. User-Property sales in PixelProperty follow the free market. Similar to other collectables, the price of User-Properties are determined by free market supply and demand

PPT TOKEN DETAILS

  1. PixelPropertyToken(PPT) is the ERC20 cryptocurrency of PixelProperty
  2. PPT has no pre-mine. All coins are generated based on Property interaction
  3. PPT inflation fluctuates, with a potential maximum of 20,000 PPT per hour
  4. PPT used to update the canvas is burned
  5. Properties in Private mode burn 2 Tokens per hour
