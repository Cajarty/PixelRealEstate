What is PixelProperty?

PixelProperty is a constantly evolving canvas of a tradable digital property where the owners of property may upload any image they like, or rent their property to other users.

What is Digital Property?

Digital properties in PixelProperty are tradable blockchain tokens powered by Ethereum. Each property is a separate individual token, which can have its appearance altered to its owners desire. Where Bitcoin and Ethereum are cryptocurrencies, these are cryptocollectables. Cryptocollectables can be bought, sold, or traded like any collectable item.
