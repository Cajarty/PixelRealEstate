PixelProperty is a free to use digital and public art canvas which rewards owners and users who join together to play, design and trade.

PixelProperty will be the world's first physical art canvas built using blockchain technology. The canvas is divided into ten thousand regions known as Properties, which allow users to show what they like. Users are rewarded in our cryptocurrency (PixelPropertyToken) for sharing and contributing to the canvas.

As a PixelProperty user, you may draw pictures or upload images as contributions to the online canvas, hosted at PixelProperty.io, allowing you to share your art with the world. We welcome all visual expressions, from an individual's art to an organization’s visual display. The digital canvas rewards users with PixelPropertyToken, which can be used to own Properties and to protect your creations.

Owning Properties gives you more control over their area. You can buy, sell and trade Properties on the built-in Property market, allowing ownership of properties to easily change hands. Properties advertise the website and message of their owners choosing, allowing them to be effectively used for personal expression or marketing. As an owner, you are rewarded in PixelPropertyToken for opening your Property up to other artists, or can choose to pay cryptocurrency to keep your Property private, giving you full control for more focused marketing.

PixelProperty is the first of its kind, attempting to not only bring a visual representation to blockchain assets, but take it one step further, showcasing a physical representation of these blockchain assets to the real world.

Founded: 2018
Location: Vancouver, BC
Funding: Sustainable Revenue Model, rather than an ICO
